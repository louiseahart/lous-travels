I love to Travel!

This year I have travelled around the Americas . . .

My journey began in my hometown London where I boarded a flight to Cancun, Eastern Mexico. From Cancun I was able to visit many little towns and cities across the Yucatan Peninsula; I experienced the tropical beach town of Tulum, the colonial city of Merida, then down to the yellow city of Uxmal and finally to the carribean islands of Isla Holbox and Isla Mujeres.
After exploring the Yucatan, I took a flight from Mexico City down to Costa Rica in Central America. As it was monsoon season, I stayed in Nosara in the Guanacaste region: This is the dryest provinence in Costa Rica due to it being on the Pacific coast.
After seeing the stunning sunsets on the beaches of Costa Rica, I flew down to Lima, Peru in South America. Here I visited the subtropical, Peruvian desert and completed a 4 day hike to Machu Picchu. I ended my hike by watching the sunrise over the famous ruins.
